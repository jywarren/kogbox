About Kogbox

Kogbox was created by Jeffrey Warren of Vestal Design. Jeff also created ARMSFLOW.org and is a co-founder of Weardrobe.

To contact him please email jeff@kogbox.com


If you experience problems with the site, please contact support@kogbox.com so we can get them fixed! We're eager to help!


Kogbox uses CodePress for syntax highlighting in Firefox and IE, and the excellent Silk icon set from FAMFAMFAM. Alex Gorbatchev's SyntaxHighlighter is also used.
